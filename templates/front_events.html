    • Matka

      Quark 2017 is proud to present the Biggest College Gaming Event in India - MATKA, for games like CS:GO and DOTA 2 with a prize pool worth ₹ 3 Lacs. We are proud to associate with SoStronk, BenQ, Gaming Central and Source as our event partners. The event consists of online qualifiers for CS-GO and DOTA 2 scheduled from 10th -15th January 2017.

      CS- GO qualifiers will be hosted on SoStronk server while DOTA 2 will be hosted on source servers. The top 8 teams for each game will qualify for the LAN finals, 4 more teams will join them in the finals selected through Goan Qualifiers (Exclusively for Goans). The dates for the LAN finals are 3rd to 5th February 2017 and will happen during Quark 2017 at BITS PILANI K.K. Birla Goa Campus.

      • Burnout

        BURNOUT is an RC car-racing event. The teams are required to fabricate a remote controlled vehicle that is powered by an Internal Combustion(IC) Engine for propulsion capable of racing on an off-road dirt track paved by a varied range of obstacles. Here, both speed and control are important as the track has biting turns, cross bumps and rough covers.
